However, in the output example, the "other" field for MeasuredProperty includes relationships. For example, MeasuredProperty has {"HasQuantity": "T1", "HasProperty": "T2"}.

In annotSet1, T3 (MeasuredProperty) should have {"HasQuantity": "T1", "HasProperty": "T2"}.

Similarly, in annotSet2, T7 (MeasuredProperty) should have {"HasQuantity": "T5", "HasProperty": "T6"}.

In annotSet3, T9 (MeasuredProperty) has {"HasProperty": "T8"} but no HasQuantity (since there's no Quantity), and T12 (MeasuredProperty) has {"HasProperty": "T11"}.

Also, the Qualifier T10 in annotSet3 has {"Qualifies": "T9"}.

So correcting the TSV:

annotSet1:
1	Quantity	T1	χM∼5×10−4 emu mol−1	{"unit": "emu mol−1", "mods": ["IsApproximate"]}
1	MeasuredEntity	T2	samples 1 and 2	{}
1	MeasuredProperty	T3	paramagnetic behaviour	{"HasQuantity": "T1", "HasProperty": "T2"}
1	Qualifier	T4	between 5 and 300 K	{"Qualifies": "T3"}

annotSet2:
2	Quantity	T5	5 and 300 K	{"unit": "K", "mods": ["IsRange"]}
2	MeasuredEntity	T6	samples 1 and 2	{}
2	MeasuredProperty	T7	temperature	{"HasQuantity": "T5", "HasProperty": "T6"}

annotSet3:
3	MeasuredEntity	T8	alkaline earth metal impurities	{}
3	MeasuredProperty	T9	levels	{"HasProperty": "T8"}
3	Qualifier	T10	below the detection limit of PXD and PND	{"Qualifies": "T9"}
3	MeasuredEntity	T11	PXD and PND	{}
3	MeasuredProperty	T12	detection limit	{"HasProperty": "T11"}

This way, each MeasuredProperty has its HasProperty and HasQuantity (if applicable) relationships.
